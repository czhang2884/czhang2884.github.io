About

I’m a statistician (M.S. Statistics) with experience working on clinical studies and healthcare research involving both structured and unstructured data. My work has included cleaning and preparing study data, building and evaluating statistical models, generating tables and figures, and creating dashboards to help teams explore results more clearly. I enjoy combining classical statistical methods with data science techniques when appropriate, and I’ve contributed analyses to peer-reviewed clinical publications. I’m most interested in roles where I can take ownership of analyses and work closely with subject-matter experts to turn data into interpretable results.
